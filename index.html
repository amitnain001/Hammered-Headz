About Us

Deep in the murky depths of the metaverse a school of thirsty predators has formed. Each Hammered Head is unique with different skins, faces, hats, and more. In addition to the unparalleled artwork, the team will be donating 20% of mint proceeds to help save the sharks! The team will be planning virtual meetups, a metaverse gathering space, merch, partnerships with other collections and lots more to bring utility and further the community’s mission of supporting sea life and cleaning our oceans.
